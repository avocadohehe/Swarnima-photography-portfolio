About Me

Hello! My name is Swarnima and I am a first-year MSBTE student currently pursuing my diploma. I have a strong interest in creativity and visual arts. Photography is one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to capture beautiful moments, nature, and everyday life through my perspective. Along with photography, I also enjoy art and creative design. I like exploring different styles, colors, and compositions that help me express ideas visually. Creating this photography portfolio website is part of my learning journey where I combine my interest in technology and creativity. Through this website, I want to showcase my passion for photography and share the beauty of captured moments with others while also improving my web development skills.
